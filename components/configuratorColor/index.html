It’s time to choose your colours.

There are a couple of things you need to keep in mind. Colours look different on every screen you view them on, so order some actual samples before making your final decision. Something else to remember is that because concrete is a natural material with natural variations, the concrete laid in your space probably won’t match the sample exactly.

The use of different sealers will impact on the final colour, as you’ll see from the swatches we supply.
