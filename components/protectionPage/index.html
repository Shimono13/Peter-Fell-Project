Choose your protection.

Your coloured concrete is laid. Now it’s time to protect it from staining and marking.

Sealing or polishing the concrete surface makes it easy to clean, maintain and prolongs the life of your concrete. The sealer you choose has an impact on the final look and feel, it also provides the finishing touch to your space.

The use of different sealers will impact on the final colour, as you’ll see from the swatches we supply.
