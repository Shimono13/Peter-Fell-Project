Here’s where you can choose the style of cuts you want in your concrete to avoid the risk of cracking.

Construction Cuts are essential in controlling concrete cracking. Talk to your contractor about where the construction cuts will be positioned. Add Decorative Cuts to add even more visual interest to your coloured concrete flooring.

Concrete cuts.

Construction cuts only

This technique is required for concrete floors to control the cracking so it occurs at the cuts.

Decorative cuts

Square pattern cuts applied in addition to construction cuts for aesthetic purposes.

Not Applicable

Generally, no cuts are needed in concrete walls, however please be aware however that for cast in-situ walls, you’ll need to include expansive joints to allow for movement. We recommend that you discuss this with your engineer.
